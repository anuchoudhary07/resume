ACADEMIC PROJECTS

MK-27 (MOSQUITO KILLER)

It is a boat like structure model with square wheels to suppress hyacinth or any other obstacle in its way, and has a sprayer with a larvicide. Worked in a team of 5 to come up with the model. Software used: Arduino.

AUTOMATIC DUSTBIN

It is a combination of a device which can sense trash when brought close to it (enabling it to open the lid) and display the level of trash automatically. Software used: Arduino.
